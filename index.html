About

🙏 Sabaidee (ສະບາຍດີ) is a commonly used expression in Lao cultures and is a warm greeting to strangers, friends, and family alike. Also, the very first lesson in HTML is printing the phrase "Hello, World!" to the World Wide Web. And in combining the two ideas, Sabaidee World was born, welcome! Follow along with me as I share codeblocks I'm learning and updates from my life as a Lao American. 

 
1. <!DOCTYPE html>
2. <html>
3. <head>
4. <title>Sabaidee</title>
5. </head>
6. <body>
7. <h1>Hello (Sabaidee) World!</h1>
8. </body>
9. </html>
